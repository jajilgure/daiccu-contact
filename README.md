# Privacy Policy for **Daiccu**

1. Data Collection: **Daiccu** does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data recorded (emojis and events) is stored strictly on the user's local device.
2. Third-Party Access: We do not provide any user data to third-party services.
3. App Permissions: **Daiccu** may request minimal permissions necessary for its core features, and no data from these permissions is transmitted outside the device.
4. In-App Purchases and Payment Data: All in-app purchases are processed directly by Apple Inc. through the App Store. The developer of daiccu does not collect, store, or have access to any financial information, such as credit card numbers or billing details. All transaction data is securely handled by Apple's payment system.
